Backyard Games Tournament Generator

Create a fair tournament schedule where teams rotate through different games and play against different opponents each round.

Enter Players

Enter player names (one per line). Each player from Group A will be paired with a player from Group B to form teams.



Pre-select Teams (Optional)

Click on two player names to pair them as a team. Anyone not assigned to a team will be randomly paired.

Number of Games

Enter the total number of different games available for the tournament. Each team will rotate through playing different games against different opponents.
